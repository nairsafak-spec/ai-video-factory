# Project Vision — AI Video Factory

## Mission

Build a self-hosted, open-source system that turns ideas into finished videos automatically — planning, generating, narrating, and editing content end to end with minimal human intervention.

## Vision

A fully autonomous AI Video Factory that consistently produces high-quality video content on its own infrastructure, learns from its results, and improves over time — owned and operated entirely by us, with no dependence on closed platforms.

## Core Principles

- **Independence** — a standalone project with no ties to any previous system.
- **Open source first** — prefer open, self-hostable tools over proprietary services.
- **Self-hosted** — the system runs on our own server, under our control.
- **Quality over speed** — correct, maintainable output matters more than shipping fast.
- **Modularity** — every component is independent and replaceable.
- **Documented decisions** — every important choice is recorded and explained.

## Long-term Goals

- Achieve end-to-end automation from topic to published video.
- Maintain a durable knowledge base that informs every production.
- Build a learning loop that measures performance and improves output.
- Scale production without a proportional increase in manual effort.
- Reach reliable, unattended operation with only high-level oversight.

## Success Criteria

- Videos are produced end to end without manual steps.
- Output quality is consistent and meets a defined standard.
- The system runs reliably and recovers from failures on its own.
- Components can be replaced or upgraded without disrupting the pipeline.
- Measurable quality and performance improve over time.

## Non-Goals

- No dependency on closed or proprietary platforms where an open alternative exists.
- No reliance on third-party managed infrastructure for core operations.
- No connection to or reuse of prior projects (e.g. NEXORA, EPOXAR).
- Not optimizing for raw output speed at the expense of quality.
- Not pursuing features that cannot run on our own server.
